Don't Hit Me!'

❮ ❯

More Info:

Developed using Unreal Engine and Android Studio, the game requires players to reach their destination within a set time limit while avoiding pedestrians and various obstacles. This blend of urgency and precision provides an engaging and exhilarating gameplay experience.

You have to get to work on time! You have been late to work several times and this is the last chance you have! Make sure you reach your destination within the time frame to advance in-game and discover new levels and earn in-game currency. With said currency, you can unlock perks, vehicles and more!

Game Mechanics:

  • Health- Avoid collisions to maintain health.
  • Time- Reach your destination before the time runs out.
  • Fuel- Manage fuel to avoid running out.
  • Objective- Get to work on time before health, time, or fuel reaches zero.
  • Game Over- Hitting pedestrians or too many objects results in game over.
  • Controls- Use a touchscreen virtual joystick and buttons for intuitive gameplay.

Unlock new levels and earn in-game currency to access perks, vehicles, and more!

My role in the development of this game consisted of gameplay mechanics and being the main UI programmer.

Rolling Ball

❮ ❯

More Info:

This game puts you in control of a smooth-rolling ball that you can move freely across the map. Your mission is to explore the area, avoid obstacles, and collect scattered objects that reward you with points.

The main goal is to gather as many points as possible within a set time limit. Each collectible item boosts your score, encouraging you to move quickly and efficiently. The more you collect, the higher your final score.

As time ticks down, players must plan their movements, avoid traps, and make the most of the available space. It's a fast-paced, casual challenge designed to test your reflexes, awareness, and strategy.

Whether you're aiming to beat your personal best or top the leaderboard, this game offers a fun and simple way to improve your focus and coordination with every round.
